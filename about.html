Introduction

This is my first semester at Amherst College as a transfer student. I am currently a junior and am aiming for a degree in Computer Science. Before moving to Amherst, I attended De Anza College in California where I completed my Associates' degree in Computer Science.

A Brief Life Recap

  • I was born in Karachi, Pakistan and am the youngest in my family.
  • I spent about 15 years living in the U.A.E and I grew up learning 3 different languages.
  • At school, I learned English and Arabic and at home I learned Urdu with my parents.
  • After graduating highschool at 17, I was lucky enough to pursue my undergraduate studies abroad which is how I ended up in the U.S!
  • I attended De Anza in California because I have family there and community college is pretty affordable.
  • I was initially a bionegineering major, but developed a deep interest in computer science after taking my first programming course in C++.
  • Along with being a student, I was also a math tutor for 2 years at my college's tutoring center.
  • Being a tutor allowed me to meet a lot of cool people and makes me want to become a teacher one day!

I believe that my experiences growing up of being an immigrant and an international student in the U.S have allowed me to learn a lot more about myself and the world at a faster pace and I am very grateful for it all.
